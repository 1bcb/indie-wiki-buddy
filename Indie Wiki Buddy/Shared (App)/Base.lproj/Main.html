Thank you for installing Indie Wiki Buddy for Safari on iOS!

⚠️ To enable this extension...
Settings → Safari → Extensions

Here, you can also grant IWB access to websites you want IWB to run on (fandom.com, google.com, etc.)

If you don't enable website access beforehand, you can enable it while browsing. Select the options menu ("AA") in your search bar, select Indie Wiki Buddy, and follow the prompts to grant permission.
